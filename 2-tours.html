Pedicab Tours

Choose Your Adventure!

What do you wanna do? What would you like to see? Where would you like to go? Join us for a tour on a pedicab! Each of our pedicab drivers are well-trained ambassadors in the city of Austin. Have them chauffeur you and your family, friends, clients and colleagues around the downtown area to find the ins and outs, secrets and not so secret parts that make this town tick.

Wanna do lunch or dinner the Austin-way? Try the our food trailer tour, the Texas Taco Tour. Looking more for food AND drinks? Book the Margarita Mixer or Texas Wine & Dine tour. Like dive bars? We got a tour for that. You can customize your own tour, too. Tell us what you want, and we'll make it happen.

Texas Taco Tour

Explore the Food Trailer scene for an Austin staple - TACOS!! We'll show you classic Tex-Mex and a infusion spin on this tasty treat, and finish with dessert and drinks

Tour is approximately 2 hours. Must take place during food trailer hours of operation. Price is based on number of pedicabs used. Each pedicab has a max capacity of 3 and max weight of 600 lbs.

Pricing
  • 1-3 Butts:     $120
  • 4-6 Butts:     $210
  • 7-9 Butts:     $300

Margarita Mixer

We show you margaritas done 3 different ways. Begin at 508 Tequila Bar with the classic. Scoot on over to Rio Rita's for Strawberry Jalapeno Ritas. And finish for dinner at the Cedar Door, the birthplace of the Mexican Martini.

Tour is approximately 2 hours. Must take place during bar hours of operation. 21 and older only. Price is based on number of pedicabs used. Each pedicab has a max capacity of 3 people and max weight of 600 lbs.

Pricing
  • 1-3 Butts:     $120
  • 4-6 Butts:     $210
  • 7-9 Butts:     $300
