Pedicab Tours

Choose Your Adventure!

What do you wanna do? What would you like to see? Where would you like to go? Join us for a tour on a pedicab! Each of our pedicab drivers are well-trained ambassadors in the city of Austin. Have them chauffeur you and your family, friends, clients and colleagues around the downtown area to find the ins and outs, secrets and not so secret parts that make this town tick.

Wanna do lunch or dinner the Austin-way? Try the our food trailer tour, the Texas Taco Tour. Looking more for food AND drinks? Book the Margarita Mixer or Texas Wine & Dine tour. Like dive bars? We got a tour for that. You can customize your own tour, too. Tell us what you want, and we'll make it happen.
